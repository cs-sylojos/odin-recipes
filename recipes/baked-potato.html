Baked Potato

Description

This baked potato has a golden crispy skin and fluffy on the inside. It's delicious and can be made with just a few simple ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium size potato
  • 1 teaspoon of olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 teaspoons of butter
  • 1 pinch of freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/4 cup of shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 freshly chopped spring onion

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 300 degrees F (150 degrees C).
  2. Scrub the potato, and pierce the skin several times with a knife or fork.
  3. Rub the skin with olive oil and then with salt.
  4. Place the potato in the preheated oven and bake until slightly soft and golden brown, about 90 minutes.
  5. Slice the potato down the center, and serve with butter and black pepper. Sprinkle shredded Cheddar cheese over the top.
  6. Garnish with additional fresh spring onion and serve immediately.
