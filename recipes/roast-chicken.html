Roast Chicken

Description

Roasting a whole chicken may look tedious, but surprisingly it's easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of onion powder, or to taste
  • 1/2 cup of butter or margarine
  • 1 stalk of celery, leave removed

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Place chicken in a roasting pan. Season generously inside and out with salt, pepper and onion powder.
  3. Place 3 tablespoons of butter in chicken cavity; rub the remaining butter on the outside of the chicken. Cut celery into 3 or 4 pieces and place in the chicken cavity.
  4. Bake the chicken uncovered in the preheated oven for about 2 hours.
  5. Remove from the oven and baste with drippings. Cover with aluminium foil and let rest for about 30 minutes before serving.
